Overview of RUHS CUET Counselling

The Rajasthan University of Health Sciences (RUHS) conducts the centralized RUHS CUET Counselling for admissions into various undergraduate health science and allied programs across the state. This online counselling process is the primary gateway for securing a seat in top government and private medical, pharmacy, and nursing institutions in Rajasthan.

Admissions are granted based on the merit of the candidate's performance in the CUET examination. The RUHS CUET Counselling process involves several key stages, including online registration, document verification, choice filling, and seat allotment. It is designed to be a transparent and efficient method for students to find the right college and branch based on their score and category ranks. The process caters to both Rajasthan domicile candidates (State Quota) and students from other states through the All India Quota.

This website provides a comprehensive guide to navigate every step of the RUHS CUET Counselling, from understanding the eligibility criteria to the final admission process. Stay updated with the latest schedules, cutoff trends, and expert tips to maximize your chances of success.

Eligibility Criteria for RUHS CUET 2026

For UG Health Science Courses (B.Pharm/B.Sc Nursing)

  • Applicants must have completed 12th grade with Physics, Chemistry, and Biology/Mathematics as compulsory subjects.
  • Minimum 50% marks in qualifying exam (45% for SC/ST/PwD).
  • Must have a valid CUET 2026 score in relevant subjects.
  • Final exam appearing candidates are also eligible.

For Paramedical & Other Allied Courses

  • Applicants must have completed or appeared in 12th grade with science stream.
  • Minimum 45% marks in qualifying exam.
  • Valid CUET 2026 score required for admission.

RUHS CUET Counselling 2026: Step-by-Step Process

The RUHS CUET Counselling is conducted entirely online. Candidates must follow these crucial steps to participate and secure admission:

  • Step 1: Online Registration: The first step is to register on the official RUHS portal. You will need your CUET application number, a valid email ID, and a mobile number to create your profile for the RUHS CUET Counselling.
  • Step 2: Application Fee Payment: After basic registration, you must pay the non-refundable counselling and part-tuition fee. This can be paid online through Net Banking, Credit Card, or Debit Card.
  • Step 3: Document Verification: Candidates must get their documents verified. This is typically done online by uploading scanned copies of all required documents. Ensure all documents are clear and in the specified format.
  • Step 4: Choice Filling and Locking: This is the most critical phase of the RUHS CUET Counselling. Log in to your account and fill in your preferred colleges and branches in order of priority. You can add, remove, or reorder your choices until the deadline. It is crucial to lock your choices to finalize them.
  • Step 5: Seat Allotment: The university will process the choices and allot seats based on your CUET score, category, and the choices you locked. The result of the seat allotment will be published on the official website.
  • Step 6: Reporting to Allotted Institute: If you are satisfied with the allotted seat, you must download the allotment letter and report to the respective institute for final admission, fee payment, and document submission within the given timeframe.

Documents Required for RUHS CUET Admission

Candidates must produce the following original documents along with self-attested copies at the time of reporting to the allotted institute:

  • Aadhaar Card or valid Photo ID proof.
  • 10th Class Marksheet and Certificate (Date of Birth Proof).
  • 12th Class Marksheet and Certificate.
  • CUET 2026 Score Card and Admit Card.
  • Rajasthan Domicile Certificate (if applicable).
  • Category Certificate (SC/ST/OBC/PwD/EWS) issued by competent authority.
  • Income Certificate (for EWS/Fee Waiver candidates).
  • Medical Fitness Certificate.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

1. What is RUHS CUET Counselling?

RUHS CUET Counselling is a centralized, online admission process conducted by the Rajasthan University of Health Sciences for admission to various health science courses in colleges across the state based on CUET scores.

2. Who is eligible for RUHS CUET Counselling?

Candidates who have appeared for the CUET exam and meet the academic eligibility criteria (passing 12th grade with required subjects and marks) are eligible to participate in the counselling.

3. Is there a separate exam for RUHS CUET Counselling?

No, there is no separate entrance exam for these seats. Admissions through RUHS CUET Counselling are based on the scores obtained in the CUET examination.

4. Can students from outside Rajasthan apply?

Yes, students from other states can apply. A certain percentage of seats are reserved under the All India Quota for candidates who are not domiciliaries of Rajasthan.

5. What happens if I don't lock my choices?

If you fail to lock your choices before the deadline, the last saved choices will be automatically locked by the system at the specified time. It is always recommended to review and lock your choices manually to avoid any errors.
