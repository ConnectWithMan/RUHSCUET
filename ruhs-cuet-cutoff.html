Overview of RUHS CUET Cutoff

The RUHS CUET Cutoff is the minimum score or rank a candidate must achieve in the CUET examination to secure admission into undergraduate health science courses (like B.Pharm, B.Sc Nursing, Paramedical) in colleges affiliated with the Rajasthan University of Health Sciences.

The official cutoffs are typically released as "Closing Ranks" alongside the seat allotment results for each counselling round. Candidates scoring above the closing rank for their respective category will be allocated a seat in the preferred institution.

Factors Determining the Cutoff

The RUHS CUET cutoff changes dynamically every year. The competent authority determines the closing ranks based on several variables:

  • Difficulty Level of Exam: If the CUET exam is tougher than usual, the overall cutoffs tend to drop.
  • Total Number of Applicants: A higher volume of candidates participating in the RUHS counselling increases competition, driving cutoffs higher for top colleges like SMS Medical College.
  • Seat Matrix Changes: The addition of new colleges or changes in the intake capacity of existing courses directly affects closing ranks.
  • Category Reservation Rules: The specific reservation quotas implemented by the Rajasthan state government strongly dictate category-wise cutoff deviations.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

1. When will the official RUHS CUET cutoff be released?

The official cutoffs and closing ranks are typically released after the completion of each seat allotment round during the counselling process.

2. Is the cutoff different for government and private colleges?

Yes, government colleges generally have a much higher cutoff (safe score) compared to private institutions due to lower fees and higher demand.

3. What is a safe score for B.Sc Nursing in a Government College?

While it varies yearly, a score of 550+ (for Unreserved category) is generally considered a safe score for securing a government nursing college.

4. Where can I check the official closing ranks?

Once released, candidates can check the official category-wise closing ranks on the official RUHS portal (ruhsraj.org) under the counselling section.
