How to Use the Predictor

Navigating the counselling process is much easier when you have realistic expectations. Follow these simple steps to use the predictor effectively:

  • Step 1: Enter your exact or expected CUET 2026 score in the first input field.
  • Step 2: Select your specific reservation category as it directly impacts your seat allotment due to the State Quota rules.
  • Step 3: Choose your preferred course (e.g., B.Sc Nursing, B.Pharm) from the dropdown list.
  • Step 4: Click on the "Predict My Colleges" button. The tool will generate a list of colleges categorized by "High Chances", "Moderate Chances", and "Tough Chances".

Factors Influencing College Allotment

While our predictor provides a highly educated estimate, the actual seat allotment by RUHS depends on several fluctuating variables each year:

  • Difficulty Level of Exam: If the CUET exam is tougher than usual, the overall cutoffs tend to drop.
  • Number of Applicants: A higher volume of candidates applying for RUHS counselling increases competition for top colleges like SMS Medical College.
  • Seat Matrix Changes: The addition of new colleges or changes in the intake capacity of existing courses directly affects closing ranks.
  • Candidate Preferences: Popular courses (like B.Sc Nursing in Govt. Colleges) generally see much higher cutoffs compared to private institutions.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

1. How accurate is the RUHS CUET College Predictor?

The predictor uses historical cutoff data and seat matrix trends to provide a highly probable estimate, though actual results may vary slightly based on current year dynamics.

2. Is this predictor free to use?

Yes, the RUHS CUET College Predictor is completely free for all candidates to use as many times as needed to plan their choice filling.

3. What details are required to use the predictor?

You need your estimated or actual CUET score/rank, your reservation category (UR/OBC/SC/ST/EWS), and your preferred course of study.

4. Does the predictor guarantee admission?

No, it only provides an estimation based on past trends. Final admission depends strictly on the official RUHS seat allotment process and merit list.
