Overview of the RUHS CUET Timeline

The RUHS CUET 2026 Counselling Schedule is highly time-sensitive. Candidates aiming for undergraduate medical, pharmacy, nursing, and paramedical programs in Rajasthan must strictly adhere to the timelines provided by the Rajasthan University of Health Sciences (RUHS). Missing a deadline could result in immediate disqualification from the admission process.

The process begins with online registration in May, followed by document verification, choice filling, and seat allotment rounds stretching through July.

Important Instructions Regarding Deadlines

To avoid cancellation of your hard-earned admission, please keep the following rules in mind:

  • Choice Locking: Choices will be auto-locked if you forget to lock them manually by the end of the deadline (July 8, 2026). Once locked, they cannot be edited.
  • Physical Reporting: Physical reporting at the allotted institute is mandatory. If you do not report within the timeframe (July 13 - 18, 2026), your allotment will be revoked, and you will not be allowed to participate in upward movement.
  • Fee Payment: All part-tuition fee deposits and registration fees must clear the bank server by 11:59 PM on the final date of registration. Avoid last-minute server delays.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

1. When does the RUHS CUET 2026 registration start?

The online registration for RUHS CUET 2026 counselling is tentatively scheduled to begin on 14 May 2026.

2. What is the deadline for choice locking?

Candidates must complete their choice filling and lock their preferences by 8 July 2026. If not locked manually, they will be auto-locked at the deadline.

3. When will the Round 1 allotment results be declared?

The Round 1 seat allotment results will be published on the official portal on 12 July 2026.

4. Can I report to the college after the given deadline?

No, physical reporting and document verification must be completed within the stipulated timeline (13 July - 18 July 2026). Failing to report will result in the cancellation of your allotted seat.

5. What if I miss the registration deadline?

Typically, the university does not accept late registrations. However, you might be eligible to register freshly if a Mop-up Round or stray vacancy round is announced later in the year.
